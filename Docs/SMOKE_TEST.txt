Voidovia — Act 1 smoke test (10 steps).

Open Assets/_Project/Scenes/Bootstrap.unity (or WorldMap.unity) and press Play. Pass = each
step happens with no console errors and no blank/frozen Game view.

1. Title screen appears ("VOIDOVIA"). Click through. Main menu appears with New Game and
   Continue (Continue is disabled/labelled "no save yet" unless a save already exists from
   a previous pass). Tap New Game (or Continue — skip to step 9 if so).
2. Character creation UI appears. Fill it in and confirm. World map opens. Log shows the
   welcome lines ("Butter rogues took it...", "Tip: use Save / Load...").
3. Select Greyledger, tap Travel, then tap Advance inch until you arrive.
4. At Greyledger, tap Advisor. Log names Ashpond and Tollbar as leads; quest beat moves to
   InvestigateCities.
5. Travel to either Ashpond or Tollbar (either is fine — picking the wrong one still
   redirects you). On arrival, intel resolves automatically and the Buttery Lair node
   appears on the map near the correct city; quest beat is LairSpawned.
6. Travel to the Buttery Lair node.
7. Tap Quest while at the lair to start the raid battle. Win AND capture the Buttery Chief
   (a win without capture does not advance the quest).
8. Quest beat is now ChiefCaptured; log confirms the heirloom was recovered.
9. Travel to Greyledger, Bastion Holt, or Red Knoll and tap Quest again. The Void Audience
   panel opens — pick Accept (mercenary contract) or Refuse.
10. Quest beat is Completed. Tap Save, then Load, and confirm hero name, gold, quest beat,
    and location all match what you had before loading.

Faster manual pass: DebugSliceHud (the IMGUI panel in the top-left corner during Play) has
buttons that shortcut steps 3-9 without needing to drive the map UI — useful for quickly
re-checking quest/battle logic after a script change.
